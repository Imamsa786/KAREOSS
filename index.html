About KARE OSS

KARE Open Source Society (KARE OSS) is the official open-source and innovation club of Kalasalingam Academy of Research and Education (KARE). The club aims to foster a culture of collaboration, creativity, and hands-on learning among students. Through hackathons, workshops, coding competitions, and community projects, KARE OSS empowers students to explore emerging technologies, contribute to open-source projects, and build practical skills for the future. Our mission is to create a vibrant tech community where innovation thrives and knowledge is freely shared.
